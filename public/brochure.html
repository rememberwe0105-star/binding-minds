About the Platform

What is DearGiver?

DearGiver is a modern donation platform built for Aotearoa New Zealand. We connect donors with verified charitable organisations, making every dollar transparent, trackable, and tax-deductible. Our platform never holds your funds — every donation flows directly to the charity through Stripe Connect.

🔒
Stripe Connect Payments
DearGiver never holds donor funds. Payments flow directly from donor to charity via Stripe's regulated platform, with full PCI-DSS compliance.
🏛️
NZ Charities Commission Verified
Every organisation on DearGiver is verified against the NZ Charities Commission register. CC numbers are validated before approval.
📊
Real-Time Impact Tracking
Donors see live fundraising progress, milestone notifications, and impact stories. Charities get real-time analytics dashboards.
🧾
Automatic Tax Receipts
Every donation generates an IRD-compliant tax receipt. Donors can track their 33.33% tax credit eligibility year-round.
🔔
Smart Notification System
Role-based unified notifications keep donors, charities, and admins informed with real-time updates on donations, milestones, and alerts.
📱
Fully Responsive Design
Beautiful, modern interface optimised for desktop, tablet, and mobile. Donate from anywhere, anytime, on any device.
For Charitable Organisations

Why Partner with DearGiver?

Expand your reach, reduce admin overhead, and focus on what matters — your mission.

1

Zero Setup Cost, Instant Onboarding

Apply online with your CC registration number. Once approved, complete Stripe Connect onboarding and start receiving donations immediately. No contracts, no sign-up fees, no minimum commitments.

2

Your Own Charity Dashboard

A dedicated management panel with real-time analytics: total donations, donor count, repeat donor ratio, and monthly trends. Create and manage unlimited fundraising projects with cover images, goals, and timelines.

3

Direct Fund Settlement

Donations go straight to your Stripe account — DearGiver never holds your money. Settlements are processed directly by Stripe with full transparency. Track every dollar in your finance overview.

4

Rich Public Profile

Upload logos, banners, gallery images (up to 10), and compliance documents. Showcase annual reports, financial statements, and impact reports to build donor trust and transparency.

5

Built-In Donor Discovery

Your organisation appears on DearGiver's public campaign pages, search results, and featured sections — reaching donors you wouldn't find through your own marketing channels.

💡 Platform Fee: Transparent & Fair

A small, transparent platform fee (2.5–3.5%) is applied per donation — far lower than traditional fundraising costs (typically 15–25%). You keep the vast majority of every dollar donated. No hidden charges. No monthly subscriptions.

For Donors

Why Give Through DearGiver?

Your generosity deserves transparency, security, and recognition.

1

33.33% Tax Credit — Automatically Tracked

Every donation to a registered NZ charity qualifies for the IRD's 33.33% tax credit. DearGiver generates compliant receipts and tracks your total donations year-round, so you never miss claiming your refund before March 31.

2

100% Verified Charities Only

Every organisation on DearGiver is verified against the NZ Charities Commission register. Our admin team reviews each application, checks CC numbers, and monitors ongoing compliance. You'll never wonder "is this charity legitimate?"

3

Watch Your Impact in Real Time

Live progress bars show exactly how much has been raised toward each project's goal. Receive milestone notifications when a project you supported reaches 25%, 50%, 75%, or 100% of its target. See how many donors are giving alongside you.

4

Secure, Simple 3-Step Checkout

Choose your amount → confirm details → complete payment. Powered by Stripe's bank-level encryption. One-time or recurring options available. Stripe fee coverage option lets you ensure 100% of your chosen amount reaches the charity.

5

Personal Donation Dashboard

Track your complete giving history, download receipts, and see your cumulative impact. Favourite campaigns to follow and support later. Get monthly impact reports summarising your charitable contributions.
